Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, go to the login page and click "Forgot Password". Follow the instructions sent to your email.

How do I submit a support ticket?

Navigate to the "Submit a Ticket" page, fill in the required details, and submit your request.

Who can view my submitted tickets?

Only you and administrators can view your submitted tickets.

What is the typical response time for tickets?

We aim to respond within 24 hours during business days.

Can I track the status of my ticket?

Yes! Log in to your account and visit the "View Tickets" page to check status updates.
